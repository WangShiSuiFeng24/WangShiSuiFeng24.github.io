问题

令FiF_iFi​是斐波那契数。证明下列各式：

a.∑i=1NFi=FN+2−2 a. \quad \displaystyle \sum\limits_{i=1}^{N} F_i = F_{N+2} - 2 a.i=1∑N​Fi​=FN+2​−2

b.FN<ϕN,其中ϕ=(1+5)/2 b. \quad \displaystyle F_N < \phi^N , 其中\phi = (1+\sqrt{5}) / 2 b.FN​<ϕN,其中ϕ=(1+5​)/2

解答

斐波那契数列的递推关系是：

Fi=Fi−1+Fi−2F0=1,F1=1,F2=2,F3=3,F4=5,⋯ F_{i} = F_{i-1} + F_{i-2} \\ F_{0} = 1, F_{1} = 1, F_{2} = 2, F_{3} = 3, F_{4} = 5, \cdots Fi​=Fi−1​+Fi−2​F0​=1,F1​=1,F2​=2,F3​=3,F4​=5,⋯

a.用数学归纳法，当N=1或2时，显然成立所以有假设∑i=1NFi=FN+2−2 a. \quad 用数学归纳法，当N=1或2时，显然成立 \\ \qquad 所以有假设 \sum\limits_{i=1}^{N} F_i = F_{N+2} - 2 \\ a.用数学归纳法，当N=1或2时，显然成立所以有假设i=1∑N​Fi​=FN+2​−2

∴∑i=1N+1Fi=∑i=1NFi+FN+1=FN+2−2+FN+1=FN+2+FN+1−2=FN+3−2=F(N+1)+2−2即证！\begin{aligned} \qquad \therefore \sum\limits_{i=1}^{N+1} F_i & = \sum\limits_{i=1}^{N} F_i + F_{N+1} \hspace{100cm}\\ & = F_{N+2} - 2 + F_{N+1} \\ & = F_{N+2} + F_{N+1} - 2 \\ & = F_{N+3} - 2 \\ & = F_{(N+1) + 2} - 2 \\ 即证！ \end{aligned} ∴i=1∑N+1​Fi​即证！​=i=1∑N​Fi​+FN+1​=FN+2​−2+FN+1​=FN+2​+FN+1​−2=FN+3​−2=F(N+1)+2​−2​

b.∵φ=(1+5)/2∴φ2=(1+52)2=1+5+254=3+52=φ+1\begin{aligned} b.\because \varphi & = (1+\sqrt{5}) / 2 \hspace {100cm}\\ \therefore \varphi^2 & = \left(\frac {1 + \sqrt{5}}{2}\right)^2 \\ & = \frac {1 + 5 + 2\sqrt{5}}{4} \\ & = \frac {3 + \sqrt{5}}{2} = \varphi + 1 \\ \end{aligned} b.∵φ∴φ2​=(1+5​)/2=(21+5​​)2=41+5+25​​=23+5​​=φ+1​

∴φ+1=φ2∴(φ+1)⋅φ−2=φ2⋅φ−2∴φ−1+φ−2=1\begin{aligned} \quad & \therefore \varphi + 1 = \varphi^2 \hspace{100cm}\\ & \therefore (\varphi + 1) \cdot \varphi^{-2} = \varphi^2 \cdot \varphi^{-2} \\ & \therefore \boxed{ \varphi^{-1} + \varphi^{-2} = 1} \\ \end{aligned} ​∴φ+1=φ2∴(φ+1)⋅φ−2=φ2⋅φ−2∴φ−1+φ−2=1​​

用数学归纳法，当N=1和N=2时，显然成立。所以有假设FN<φN且FN−1<φN−1 \quad 用数学归纳法，当 N=1 和N=2 时，显然成立。\\ \quad 所以有假设 F_N < \varphi^N 且 F_{N-1}< \varphi^{N-1}\\ 用数学归纳法，当N=1和N=2时，显然成立。所以有假设FN​<φN且FN−1​<φN−1

FN+1=FN+FN−1<φN+φN−1<φ−1φN+1+φ−2φN+1<(φ−1+φ−2)φN+1<φN+1即证！\begin{aligned} F_{N+1} & = F_N + F_{N-1} \hspace{100cm} \\ & < \varphi^N + \varphi^{N-1} \\ & < \varphi^{-1}\varphi^{N+1} + \varphi^{-2} \varphi^{N+1} \\ & < (\varphi^{-1} + \varphi^{-2})\varphi^{N+1} \\ & < \varphi^{N+1} \\ 即证！ \end{aligned} FN+1​即证！​=FN​+FN−1​<φN+φN−1<φ−1φN+1+φ−2φN+1<(φ−1+φ−2)φN+1<φN+1​
